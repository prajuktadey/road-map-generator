about yoda

yoda is an ai-powered learning assistant designed to help you achieve your career goals by providing personalized learning roadmaps

With yoda, you can explore new career opportunities, gain new skills, and stay ahead in your field

imagine this

feeling lost when starting to learn something new is a thing of the past. with my service, you simply drop in the job description of the role you want, and you'll get a curated list of links to paid/free courses, recent research articles, and a comprehensive roadmap, all in one place
